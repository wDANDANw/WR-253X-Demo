Equal Access to Technology Designs

Published May 1, 2022

Everyone should have equal rights and chance to convert their original ideas and thoughts to technology-assisted designs

It Here "It" refers to the time when the manifesto is formed, which is in the era that year 2022 belongs to â€“ the information age. The Information Age, or Computer Age, is a historical period that began in the mid-20th century, characterized by a rapid epochal shift from traditional industry established by the Industrial Revolution to an economy primarily based upon information technology (computers). is the best era for designing, while it is also the worst era for designing.

Technology has helped designers to bring unimaginable astonishing designs to the world, but technology is also discouraging designers to bring out designs.

If you know how to use technology, then you might be able to create astonishing arts like this. However, if you do not know about technology and if you want to enter the field, you have to be prepared to spend over tens but not hundreds of hours of time on learning before creating anything as you wished.

This should not be how it is. Design is a natural human activity that everyone has equal access to. Everyone can design and share their designs to the world. But when it comes to technology-assisted design, all changed. It seems that only technology people can design. No. Technology people should make it accessible to everyone. With greater accessibility, we would be able to see way more brilliant designs.
