Equal Access to Technology Designs

Published May 1, 2022

Everyone should have equal rights and chance to convert their original ideas and thoughts to technology-assisted designs

Background of the Manifesto

The central vision I want to convey in the manifesto is that "everyone should have equal rights and a chance to convert their original ideas and thoughts to technology-assisted designs" (techno-designs).

Read More

Assumption 1: Efforts should guarantee returns

Designers should expect that they can get a techno-design after paying a certain quantity of effort, and they would be able to polish the design with more effort.

Read More

Assumption 2: Techno-designs should grant equal accessibility to everyone

People with technology background should find means to lower the entrance level of techno-designs to ensure the accessibility of techno-designs.

Read More

Assumption 3: Greater accessibility will bring design justice and more brilliant designs

Greater accessibility will bring more brilliant designs.
